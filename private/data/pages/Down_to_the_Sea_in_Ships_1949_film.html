Down to the Sea in Ships is a 1949 seafaring drama directed by Henry Hathaway, starring Richard Widmark and Lionel Barrymore. The supporting cast includes Dean Stockwell, Cecil Kellaway, Gene Lockhart, and John McIntire.

Plot

Whaling ship captain Bering Joy takes his grandson Jed on a whaling expedition in order to teach the young boy real life values such as honesty, courage, wisdom,fairness and hard work.

Cast

  • Lionel Barrymore as Captain Bering Joy
  • Dean Stockwell as Jed Joy
  • Cecil Kellaway as Slush Tubbs
  • Gene Lockhart as Andrew Bush
  • Berry Kroeger as Manchester
  • John McIntire as Thatch
