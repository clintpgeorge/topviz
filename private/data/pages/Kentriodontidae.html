Kentriodontidae is an extinct family of odontocet whales related to modern dolphins. The Family lived from the Oligocene to the Pliocene before going extinct.

Taxonomy

The kentriodontids are grouped in three subfamilies with two genera considered incertae sedis, or of uncertain placement in the family.

  • Family Kentriodontidae
    • Subfamily Kentriodontinae
      • Belonodelphis
      • Delphinodon
      • Incacetus
      • Kentriodon (syn. Grypolithax)
      • Macrokentriodon
      • Microphocaena
      • Rudicetus
      • Tagicetus
    • Subfamily Lophocetinae
      • Hadrodelphis
      • Liolithax
      • Lophocetus
    • Subfamily Pithanodelphininae
      • Atocetus
      • Leptodelphis
      • Pithanodelphis
      • Sophianacetus (syn. Mediocris)
    • Subfamily incertae sedis
      • Sarmatodelphis
      • Kampholophos
